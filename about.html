Amadeo Drafting was founded in Montreal by Juan Jaimes to give manufacturers, engineers and architects a reliable freelance partner for precision drafting work — without the overhead of a large studio.

For years, Juan has worked on the shop floor side of drafting: steel fabrication, millwork, industrial equipment. The drawings you receive are built the way fabricators actually need them — complete, unambiguous, and constructible.

We serve clients from small metal shops and cabinetmakers to architecture studios and general contractors. Every project is handled personally, with tight feedback loops and realistic deadlines.
