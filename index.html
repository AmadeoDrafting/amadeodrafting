Amadeo Drafting & Design

Amadeo Drafting & Design provides professional 2D drafting, 3D modeling and rendering services for engineers, architects, manufacturers and construction companies.

The company is led by Juan Camilo Jaimes, a Mechanical Engineering Technician based in Canada, with over 10 years of experience in structural steel design and product development.

Juan specializes in 3D modeling, technical drafting and fabrication drawings, including civil and architectural plans, structural steel detailing and product design. He also produces high-quality 3D renderings to help visualize projects before fabrication or construction.

Amadeo Drafting & Design helps transform ideas into clear, precise and fabrication-ready drawings, supporting engineers, fabricators and businesses throughout the design process.
